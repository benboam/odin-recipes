Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is a one-pan dish of eggs poached in a spiced tomato-pepper sauce. You start by softening onion and bell pepper in olive oil with garlic, then stir in ground cumin, paprika (sweet or smoked), and a pinch of chili or harissa. Add crushed tomatoes (canned or fresh), a little salt, and let it simmer until thick and jammy. Make small wells in the sauce, crack in the eggs, cover, and cook just until the whites set and the yolks stay runny.

Finish with crumbled feta, a handful of herbs (parsley, cilantro, or dill), and a drizzle of good olive oil. Serve straight from the pan with warm crusty bread or pita to scoop. You can riff with extras like spinach, olives, or chickpeas; for more heat, add more chili; for richer sauce, swirl in a spoon of tomato paste. It’s simple, cozy, and perfect any time of day.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ground cumin (2 tsp)
  • paprika, sweet or smoked (1 tsp)
  • chili flakes or harissa (to taste)
  • tomato paste (1 tbsp, optional but nice)
  • crushed tomatoes, 1 × 400g/14oz can (or 4–5 ripe tomatoes, chopped)
  • salt & black pepper
  • 6 large eggs
  • feta, crumbled (optional)
  • fresh herbs: parsley/coriander/dill (pick one or mix)
  • bread or pita

Steps

  1. Prep: dice 1 onion and 1 red bell pepper; mince 3 garlic cloves.
  2. Warm 2–3 tbsp olive oil in a wide pan over medium heat.
  3. Soften onion and pepper with a pinch of salt for 6–8 min, until sweet.
  4. Stir in garlic for 30 seconds.
  5. Add 1–2 tsp cumin, 1 tsp paprika, a pinch of chilli/harissa, and 1 tbsp tomato paste; cook 30–60 seconds to toast.
  6. Stir in 1 × 400g can crushed tomatoes (or 4–5 chopped fresh) plus 50–100 ml water if needed; season with ½ tsp salt and black pepper; simmer 8–12 min until thick and jammy.
  7. Taste and adjust salt/heat. (Optional add-ins now: handful of spinach, olives, or cooked chickpeas.)
  8. Make 4–6 small wells in the sauce; crack in the eggs; sprinkle eggs with a little salt and pepper.
  9. Cover and cook 4–6 min for runny yolks or 7–9 min for set. (Oven option: 180°C fan, 6–10 min.)
  10. Finish with crumbled feta, chopped herbs (parsley/coriander/dill), and a drizzle of olive oil; serve with warm bread or pita.
